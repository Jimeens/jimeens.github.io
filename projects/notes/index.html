Quantum Field Theory I

Here I will be sharing my solutions for the homework assignments I had in my Quantum Field Theory course, taught by Ph.D. Gustavo Alberto Burdman at the University of São Paulo’s Institute of Physics (IFUSP) during the first semester of 2023. In this course, the professor Burdman provides all his class notes in Quantum Field Theory I - 1st semester 2023. I consider this notes very good to follow the course, and as a complementary resources, I used Peskin & Schröder, Sredninki, Schwarz, and other books which I think are good books to study QFT. Personally, I consider it to be one of the best courses I have taken in my undergraduate studies.

Quantum Mechanics II

This is my lecture notes for the course of Quantum Mechanics II, which I am taking with Ph.D. Alexandre A. do Passo Suaide at the University of São Paulo's Institute of Physics (IFUSP) during the first semester of 2023. These notes are a combination of my lecture notes, supplemented with additional material from various sources, such as extra bibliography, and revised notation and constants as needed. These lecture notes are a continuation of Quantum Mechanics I, so the intention is still to publish a book with both of them combined. Note that this one is still in development.
*Co-author*: Felipe Gimenez Souza
